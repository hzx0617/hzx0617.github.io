  1. CIKM
    LENS: Leveraging Explainable Skills for Listwise Evaluation in Kuaishou Search
    Zhongxin Huang* and others
    Proceedings of the 32nd ACM International Conference on Information and Knowledge Management (CIKM), 2026
    Under review
    Abs Bib

    A skill-based whole-page search quality evaluation framework enabling automated GSB evaluation and bad-case attribution. We propose a two-stage SFT + DAPO-based training scheme whose offline classification accuracy and attribution recall clearly surpass mainstream LLM baselines. Online human spot-check consistency reached 84.2%, with evaluation efficiency 40.6x over manual annotation on L20 machines.

  1. CompNet
    SPDTM: A Shapley-value Based Preference Discovery and Task Matching to Maximize Satisfaction in Mobile Crowd Sensing
    Zhongxin Huang, Zhijun Sun, Wenbo Tan, and 4 more authors
    Computer Networks, Oct 2025
    Abs DOI Bib

    A Shapley-value based reinforcement learning framework for preference discovery and task matching in Mobile Crowd Sensing (MCS), addressing low task-worker matching efficiency and false preference reporting. Uses a three-stage design with MADDPG and multi-armed bandit; on real datasets, user preference recognition reached 94.79% and task completion rate 91.77%, outperforming baselines.
