I am a Ph.D. student at the [School of Computer Science](https://cs.pku.edu.cn/), [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn/), where my research focuses on large language models (LLMs) and their application to search & recommendation systems. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree from the [School of Computer Science](https://cse.csu.edu.cn/), [Central South University](https://www.csu.edu.cn/), from which I was recommended for direct-entry Ph.D. admission to Peking University. I come from Longyan, Fujian, China. Outside of research, I enjoy running, playing basketball, and singing.

I am passionate about bridging academic research and real-world industry practice. During my Ph.D., I have interned at Alibaba's Qwen Business Unit and Kuaishou's Search Technology Department, where I developed LLM-driven retrieval and search algorithms for large-scale production systems and submitted several papers. Looking ahead, I am actively seeking opportunities to collaborate with both academic and industry partners on research that leads to impactful publications.

If you are interested in discussing research or exploring potential collaborations, feel free to reach out to me by email at [2294847195@qq.com](mailto:2294847195@qq.com).
